Fluffy Pancakes

Sourced from "Goshujinsama to Kemonomimi no Shoujo Mel"
Have not tested the recipe irl yet tho so results may differ from shown ∠( ᐛ 」∠)_

Very cool pancakes

This recipe is an extract from a manga chapter where the characters attempt to cook pancakes.

Pancakes are a simple yet fulfilling dish for beginners, myself included. The product of this recipe should probably be fluffly cloud bread disks so if yours aren't, I have no advise for you because I have never tried this recipe before :/

My reason for choosing this recipe is this website being a sort of love letter to the orignal manga. I look forward to the day I will try this recipe and enjoy the process and pancakes as much as I enjoyed reading the manga itself :D

Ingredients:

  • Wheat Flour, 180g
  • Sugar, 3 tablespoons
  • Salt, 1/2 teaspoon
  • Milk, 300cc
  • Baking Powder, 3 teaspoons
  • Egg, 1
  • Melted Butter, 3 tablespoons

Putting it together!

Image for yall visual learners and otakus

  1. Take flour, baking powder, sugar and salt,
    and mix them in a bowl.
  2. Take the milk, eggs and butter,
    and mix them in a separate bowl.
  3. Take (1) and (2), and mix them together,
    then pour them in the pan.
  4. When bubbles start to form on the top of the pancake,
    give it a flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  5. Repeat (1) to (4) until you have 2 to 3 pancakes,
    or have ran out of batter
